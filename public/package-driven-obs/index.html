My Role Lead Product Designer

The Team Technical Lead, 3 Senior Engineers

Timeframe 2022

Background

In early 2022,

I lead the project alongside an Engineering Technical Lead. We worked across the Observability organization as well as with the Elastic UI design system team to establish cohesion throughout the product. The following case study shows the end to end product design effort which resulted in a Saved Views feature being implemented in the Observability Logs solution.

Research

I conducted five, hour-long user interviews with a mix a of internal users, external users, and Solutions Architecrs. I used Zoom to conduct the interviews and the Dovetail platform to synthesize interviews and pull out themes.

Challenges

Guiding the user without asking leading questions that will simply validate our hypothesis. Getting feedback on the current experience while still allowing for the user to present their ideal experience.

Key Findings

  • Users want to see their data in context out of the box without having to build complex Dashboards.
  • Current experience is disjointed. Onboarding doesn't flow well into Observability solution.
  • Different roles have different needs.
  • Users range from beginners to “power users”
